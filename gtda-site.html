Our Legal Framework

GTDA operates on a firm constitutional and common-law foundation. Our notes are issued as private commodity instruments, representing title to a specific quantity of gold. They are not, and do not purport to be, official legal tender. We invite you to review our comprehensive legal position which outlines the historical precedent and legal basis for private, asset-backed notes in commerce.
