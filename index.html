A Clear Standard of Value

Our system is anchored to a transparent and consistent measure of value. Both our digital and physical instruments are directly tied to the underlying gold they represent.

  • Digital Tokens: Each digital token is representative of exactly 1/20th of a troy ounce of gold.
  • Paper Currency: We offer varying denominations of 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, and 100. Each 1 unit of face value is pegged to the same standard, representing 1/20th of a troy ounce of gold.

Our Legal Framework

GTDA operates on a firm constitutional and common-law foundation. Our notes and tokens are issued as private commodity instruments, representing title to a specific quantity of gold. They are not, and do not purport to be, official legal tender. We invite you to review our comprehensive legal position which outlines the historical precedent and legal basis for private, asset-backed instruments in commerce.
